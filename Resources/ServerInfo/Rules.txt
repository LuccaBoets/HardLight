[head=3]Hard Light is an English-speaking 18+ medium roleplay server.[/head]

[color=#a4885c][head=3]0. Implementation and interpretation of the rules is at the sole discretion of the Admins.[/head][/color]
  • Admins are held accountable through the Admin Code of Conduct.

[color=#ffff00]If you have any questions about these rules, press F1 in-game (or click AHelp in the lobby), or ask in the Discord.[/color]

[color=#00fffb]Rules shown in this color are enforced at all times, without requiring a player report.[/color]
All other rules are enforced upon receiving a player report.

[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 1: Behavioral Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]1.1[/color] Be respectful to others out-of-character.

[color=#a4885c]1.2[/color] Do not use hate speech, slurs, racism, or bigoted language.

[color=#a4885c]1.3[/color] [color=#00fffb]Do not metagame.[/color]

[color=#a4885c]1.4[/color] Do not spam or advertise.

[color=#a4885c]1.5[/color] Do not intentionally take actions that negatively impact or disrupt others' enjoyment of the game.

[color=#a4885c]1.6[/color] Avoid controversial topics such as self-harm in public channels.

[color=#a4885c]1.7[/color] [color=#00fffb]Do not evade bans.[/color]

[color=#a4885c]1.8[/color] Do not self-antag.

[color=#a4885c]1.9[/color] [color=#00fffb]Do not disconnect to avoid in-game consequences (ragequitting).[/color]

[color=#a4885c]1.10[/color] [color=#00fffb]Cheating and exploiting bugs is forbidden.[/color]

[color=#a4885c]1.11[/color] [color=#00fffb]Respect other players' out-of-character consent and preferences.[/color]


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 2: Gameplay Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]2.1[/color] [color=#00fffb]If you are banned from a role or department, you may not play that role. This includes seeking promotions into banned roles or taking ghost roles that hold the banned role.[/color]

[color=#a4885c]2.2[/color] Do not make yourself a major problem or disruption for other players while not playing as an antagonist.

[color=#a4885c]2.3[/color] [color=#00fffb]It is strictly forbidden to livestream rounds to others or share recorded gameplay outside of Hardlight without consent of all parties involved. [/color]*2

[color=#a4885c]2.4[/color] Ghosting with the sole intent of spying on others' private activities is not permitted.

[color=#a4885c]2.5[/color] [color=#00fffb]Vandalism, theft, or major disturbances on ColComm are not permitted. Trespassing within the central ColComm offices is also prohibited.[/color]


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 3: Metagaming Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]3.1[/color] Use chat channels for their intended purposes.

[color=#a4885c]3.2[/color] Characters do not retain knowledge of events that occurred while they were dead.

[color=#a4885c]3.3[/color] Do not act on information gained through out-of-character communication (meta-communication).

[color=#a4885c]3.4[/color] [color=#00fffb]discussion about the current round is allowed on the Hardlight Discord server HOWEVER acting on information gained through this method is considered metagaming. [/color]*1

[color=#a4885c]3.5[/color] Do not meta-grudge or meta-friend. Using in-character or out-of-character information to give unfair treatment or advantages to specific players is prohibited.

[color=#a4885c]3.6[/color] [color=#00fffb]Do not antag-roll. Intentionally disconnecting or entering cryo to seek antagonist roles is forbidden.[/color]


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 4: New-Life Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]4.1[/color] Players retain all in-character memory up until going critical.

[color=#a4885c]4.2[/color] If a player is revived, they may only recall vague, unclear impressions of how they died — not specific details.


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 5: Character Naming Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]5.1[/color] Do not use names that are meta-references to real people, other media, or other players.

[color=#a4885c]5.2[/color] Choose a believable and fitting name for your character. Leeway is given to theatrical roles such as the Clown, Boxer, and Mime, which may use stage names with some creative freedom.


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 6: Roleplay Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]6.1[/color] Your character is a separate entity from you, the player. Your character's actions, feelings, and knowledge should be based solely on their in-character experiences.

[color=#a4885c]6.2[/color] Do not play excessively hostile or provocative characters. Accurately portraying a character should never require breaking the rules. Use AHelp if you have questions.

[color=#a4885c]6.3[/color] If you select a job at round start, you are expected to perform that role when needed.

[color=#a4885c]6.4[/color] Do not intentionally break roleplay to gain an unfair advantage while another player is typing.

[color=#a4885c]6.5[/color] [color=#00fffb]Respect other players' consent, dignity, and boundaries. If someone asks you to stop bothering them via LOOC, do so. Abuse of this rule should be reported via AHelp immediately.[/color]

[color=#a4885c]6.6[/color] Do not dismantle key station infrastructure (e.g. escape pods, engine systems).

[color=#a4885c]6.7[/color] [color=#00fffb]Roleplaying an underage character is strictly forbidden, as is any related discussion.[/color]


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 7: Powergaming Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]7.1[/color] Do not hide antagonist objective items.

[color=#a4885c]7.2[/color] Do not make deliberate use of known bugs or exploits.


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 8: End-of-Round (EOR) Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]8.1[/color] End-of-round griefing (EORG) is not permitted under any circumstances. This includes fighting, explosives, chemicals, property destruction, or any other disruptive behavior.

[color=#a4885c]8.2[/color] The Arrivals terminal and the Evacuation shuttle (and hallway) are safe zones where this rule applies at all times.


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 10: Command and Security Rules[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]10.1[/color] Command and Security roles are held to a higher standard. You are expected to know and perform your duties to the best of your ability.

[color=#a4885c]10.2[/color] Command and Security must be familiar with Space Law, Standard Operating Procedures, Alert Procedures, and Company Policy.

[color=#a4885c]10.3[/color] Do not abuse your position of power.

[color=#a4885c]10.4[/color] You are expected to engage in roleplay with all players, including antagonists.

[color=#a4885c]10.5[/color] If you are in a Command role and need to cryo or disconnect, inform your department and Command beforehand.


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 11: Antagonist Guidelines[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]11.1[/color] Antagonists are held to a higher standard, similar to Command.

[color=#a4885c]11.2[/color] Antagonists are expected to engage in roleplay with other players, including Security and Command.

[color=#a4885c]11.3[/color] The damage an antagonist causes must be proportional to their objectives.

[color=#a4885c]11.4[/color] Ghost roles have their own independent rules that must be followed.

[color=#a4885c]11.5[/color] [color=#00fffb]All antagonistic activity must cease on ColComm.[/color]


[color=#ffff00][head=3]Section 12: Escalation Guidelines[/head][/color]

[color=#a4885c]12.1[/color] Lethal force against other players should be a last resort.

[color=#a4885c]12.2[/color] Do not disproportionately escalate in response to others' actions.

[color=#a4885c]12.3[/color] Do not ignore critical or downed players unless it is directly related to an objective.

[color=#a4885c]12.4[/color] Do not intentionally and permanently remove other players from the round unless it is directly related to an objective.
